We are a group of students passionate about the ongoing invasion, genocide, and human rights violations in Palestine, and the University of Waterloo's part in supporting it.

After the last Waterloo Undergraduate Student Association's referendum has passed, the University has done nothing to divest its relationship with Technion, the IDF's research wing.

Since they are ignoring our calls, our protests, and the loss of innocent lives in Palestine, we will shut down the school - let them try to ignore us then!

We call for the University of Waterloo to:

  • Sever ties with Technion;
  • Discontinue awards associated with Israeli institutions;
  • Divest from corporations and asset managers which are profiting from the annihilation of Gaza and the invasion of Lebanon; and,
  • End the repression of student activism.
